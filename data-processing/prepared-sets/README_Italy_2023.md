## Key Insights

1. **Coal**: Sourced from southern hemisphere (South Africa, Australia, Indonesia) and Americas (US, Colombia). No EU suppliers in top 5.

2. **Oil**: Diversified across Caspian region (Azerbaijan, Kazakhstan), North Africa (Libya), Middle East (Iraq), and US. Russia notably absent from top 5.

3. **Natural Gas**: Algeria dominates (36%) via pipeline, followed by LNG from Qatar, US, and Azerbaijan. Russia down to minimal levels post-2022.

4. **Electricity**: Imported from neighbors - Switzerland and France provide 74% combined, reflecting cross-border grid connections.

5. **Biofuels**: Most diversified supply - includes EU partners (Austria, Germany, Spain) and overseas (Indonesia, Brazil).
